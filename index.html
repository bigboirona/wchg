For higher-income households and business owners.

Advanced insurance planning is not about chasing a product. It is about coordinating protection, liquidity, tax sensitivity, legacy goals, and risk tolerance. Indexed universal life and leveraged strategies can be complex topics, but they need careful design, realistic assumptions, and a clear understanding of costs and risks.

  • Indexed universal life structure and illustration review
  • Overfunded policy design and cash value planning concepts
  • Premium financing and leveraged IUL conversations
  • Policy loan mechanics, downside risks, and stress testing
  • Legacy, estate liquidity, and business-continuity planning

Important planning note

IUL values, crediting, loans, and policy performance are not guaranteed and depend on policy terms, costs, caps, participation rates, loan rates, and ongoing funding. Leveraged strategies add repayment and interest-rate risk.

Any discussion should be reviewed against a carrier illustration and your broader tax, legal, and financial situation.
